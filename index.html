Welcome to womens health voice foundation

Despite being one of the relatively fast-growing countries in Africa, (Tanzania) still has very high maternal mortality rate. Poor maternal, newborn, and child health indicators in Tanzania particularly in rural communities is attributed by the fact that many women prefer to deliver at home rather than at the nearest health facility.
